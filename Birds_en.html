The Grey Heron

The grey heron is a bird of the pelican—like order, the heron family. The appearance is very characteristic. It is a long-legged, long-necked bird, gray above and white below, with black inclusions, with a rather long sharp beak. The size is quite large, the weight of an adult male can reach 2 kg. Females are slightly smaller than males, but otherwise they are almost the same.
